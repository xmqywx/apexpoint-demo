1 New Arrivals — Workflow Changes

Restructure the pipeline to reduce clicks and allow early decision-making.
1.1 Merge Inspect + Depollute into one step
Merge Inspect and Depollute into one "Processing" stage. Remove Decision as a separate stage — destination becomes a vehicle attribute selectable at any time (Arrived or Processing). Pipeline goes from 4 columns to 2 columns.
Arrived→ Inspect→ Depollute→ Decision
▼ becomes ▼
Arrived→ Processing (Inspect + Depollute)
✅ Destination (Parts/Recycling/Sold/Scrap) is always visible at the top of every vehicle panel. Select it anytime, then hit "Dispatch" when ready. No separate Decision step needed.
1.2 Destination is a vehicle attribute, not a pipeline step
Destination selector (Parts / Recycling / Sold / Scrap) is always visible at the top of every vehicle panel. Can be set at any time — Arrived or Processing. When destination is selected + vehicle is ready, click "Dispatch" to send it.
💡 Two paths: (1) Set destination at Arrived → Dispatch immediately (skip processing). (2) Set destination → Move to Processing → Complete inspection → Dispatch.
1.4 Add "Complete Cars in Yard" section
Show vehicles that completed new arrivals but are still in the yard. Currently these disappear from view.

Currently requires 15-20 clicks. Target: under 5 clicks for a standard vehicle.
3.1All items default to YES

3.2Simplify to Yes/No toggle
Replace 5-option buttons (Good/Fair/Poor/Missing/N/A) with simple Yes / No toggles. No per-item notes — one unified Notes section at the bottom.

8 Concept Confirmation — Please Verify

To ensure we're building the right thing, please confirm our understanding.
8.1Vehicle destination flow — is this correct?
The Decision stage routes each vehicle to ONE of these 4 destinations:
Vehicle Arrives
↓ Inspect & Depollute (or skip) ↓
Decision: Choose Destination
Parts
Strip the car, sell individual parts
Recycling
Whole car to metal recycler
Sold Complete
Sell the whole car as-is
Scrap
Scrap metal disposal
⚠️ Please confirm: Are these 4 correct? Any overlap between Recycling and Scrap?
8.2Overseas is a separate business module — correct?
Overseas is NOT a vehicle destination from New Arrivals. It is a separate module for container management, overseas buyers, and export tracking.
Overseas Module manages:
  • Container management
  • Overseas buyer database
  • Parts assigned to containers
  • Export tracking
NOT the same as:
  • Sending a whole car overseas
  • A destination in Decision
⚠️ Please confirm: Overseas will be developed separately after main vehicle flow is complete.
8.32-column pipeline with flexible dispatch — correct?
The kanban has 2 columns only: Arrived → Processing. Destination is set on the vehicle (not a separate step). Vehicle can be dispatched from either column once a destination is chosen.
Fast path (no inspection needed):
Vehicle arrives → Set destination → Dispatch immediately
Standard path:
Vehicle arrives → Move to Processing → Inspect & Depollute → Set destination → Dispatch
⚠️ Please confirm: Is this the intended workflow flexibility?
